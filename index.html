What does Care For Patients do ?

Functionalities of our Saline Tracking System

Track Saline Levels

AquaAlarm is a physical device to track saline levels and Care For Patients digitally tracks saline levels

Alert incase levels drop

AquaAlarm has a sound-based alert to indicate a drop in saline levels

Record Levels and Timings

Website has patientwise record of Saline Levels with timestamps of dosage adminstration

Efficient Procedure

Helps nurses and other healthcare professionals to carry out their duties and tasks in a seamless fashion

Better Healthcare Delivery

Overall Efficeny of Healthcare improves and Patients can be saved from serious conditions

What is The School Challenge ?

The Schools Challenge, a global initiative run by J.P. Morgan to prepare young people with the skills they need to be successful in a rapidly changing economy, is being implemented in Mumbai in collaboration with Agastya International Foundation. The Vision of Schools Challenge is to support students to make informed choices about their next steps in education and employment, through inspiring them about science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM), providing university student mentors and JP Morgan Staff to enthuse them about STEM and further education.
