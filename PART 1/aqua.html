What is Aqua Alarm ?

AquaAlarm is a hardware device created and designed to help nurses and hospital staff in delivering better healthcare. AquaAlarm works as an Alarm and alerts the nurse, hospitall staff, patient and their relatives incase the Saline level drops below the marked level

Material Used

Physical and Electronic equipment used to build AquaAlarm

XKC-Y25-V Intelligent Sensor

Non-Contact sensor to understand level of Saline

Saline Bottle

For demonsatration purposes, a small sized NaCl solution Saline Bottle

Saline Stand

Metal based saline stand which will be used in our product

Battery

Generic 9V HI-Watt Battery to give power supply for demonsatration purposes

Electronic Buzzer

Audio signaling device to alert everyone around the patient

Breadboard

To make connections and join the different electronic components

Adhesive Material

Double sided tape and other regular adhesive material

Cardboard

To make connector between sensor and saline bottle and for strengthening

Pvc Pipes

To make temporary saline stand for demonsatration purposes
